## 2025-05-15 - Improving Form Accessibility and Progress Feedback
**Learning:** Adding `for` attributes to labels and `aria-live="polite"` to status messages significantly improves the experience for screen reader users and increases the hit area for all users. Using `role="progressbar"` with `aria-valuenow` provides standardized feedback for long-running operations.
**Action:** Always ensure labels are explicitly associated with inputs and provide ARIA attributes for dynamic loading states in future projects.
