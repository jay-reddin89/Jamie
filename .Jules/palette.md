## 2025-05-14 - [Form and Modal Accessibility]
**Learning:** Adding 'for' attributes to labels not only improves screen reader support but also significantly enhances the mobile and desktop UX by increasing the clickable hit target for form inputs. Additionally, implementing the 'Escape' key listener for modals is a critical expectation for keyboard users that is often overlooked in custom implementations.
**Action:** Always ensure labels are explicitly associated with inputs and modals handle standard keyboard interactions like 'Escape' and focus trapping.
