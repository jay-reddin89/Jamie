## 2026-03-01 - Accessible Collapsible Sections
**Learning:** Converting non-semantic interactive `div` elements to `<button>` elements with `aria-expanded` and `aria-controls` attributes, combined with a themed `focus-visible` indicator, significantly improves keyboard and screen reader accessibility while maintaining visual design via a `btn-reset` utility.
**Action:** Always prefer semantic `<button>` for collapsible triggers and ensure a clear focus indicator is present for keyboard navigation.
