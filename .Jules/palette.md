## 2025-05-15 - Improving Form and Modal Accessibility

**Learning:** Standardizing form accessibility through label-input association and keyboard navigation (Escape key for modals, focus indicators) significantly lowers the barrier for users with assistive technologies and improves general usability in high-contrast dark themes.

**Action:** Always ensure <label> elements have 'for' attributes and modals implement 'Escape' key dismissal as a baseline. Use :focus-visible to provide clear indicators without cluttering the UI for mouse users.
