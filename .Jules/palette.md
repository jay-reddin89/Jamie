## 2025-05-14 - Accessibility Fundamentals: Labels and Focus
**Learning:** In highly customized dark-themed UIs, focus indicators are often suppressed for "cleanliness," and semantic associations like label-input pairings are overlooked, creating significant barriers for keyboard and screen reader users.
**Action:** Always verify that every input has an explicitly associated label and that a high-contrast :focus-visible style is present to ensure navigable paths for all users.
