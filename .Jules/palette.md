## 2026-03-19 - Improved Onboarding Form Accessibility and Clarity
**Learning:** Explicit association between `<label>` and `<input>` using the `for` attribute is critical for screen reader compatibility and significantly improves the hit target size, particularly on touch devices. Visual 'required' markers combined with HTML5 `required` attributes provide both immediate visual feedback and robust programmatic validation.
**Action:** Always ensure all form fields have explicitly linked labels and clearly communicate mandatory versus optional status to the user.
