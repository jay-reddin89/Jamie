## 2025-05-15 - Form Accessibility & Visual Indicators

**Learning:** Explicit association between `<label>` and `<input>` using the `for` attribute is critical for accessibility. It ensures that screen readers correctly identify labels and increases the clickable hit target for all users. Visual indicators like asterisks for required fields provide immediate feedback, reducing user frustration during form submission.

**Action:** Always verify that form labels are explicitly associated with their inputs. Use the HTML `required` attribute along with visual cues to clearly communicate mandatory fields.
