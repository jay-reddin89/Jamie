## 2026-03-17 - Label-Input Association & Hit Targets
**Learning:** Explicitly associating <label> elements with <input> using 'for' and 'id' attributes is a simple but powerful UX win. Beyond screen reader support, it significantly increases the interactive hit target area, making the form easier to use for everyone, especially on touch devices or for users with limited motor control.
**Action:** Always verify that every form input has a corresponding label with a matching 'for' attribute.
