## 2026-04-12 - Improving Accessibility and Form UX
**Learning:** Even in highly stylized "futuristic" dashboards, basic accessibility principles like associating labels with inputs and providing clear focus indicators are crucial for usability. Grammatical errors in labels (like "Persons Name") can break the immersion and professionalism of the interface.
**Action:** Always verify label-input associations and ensure interactive elements have distinct `:focus-visible` states, especially when using custom-styled buttons and icons.
