## 2025-05-14 - Label-Input Association for Improved Hit Targets
**Learning:** Explicit association between `<label>` and `<input>` using `for` and `id` attributes is critical for screen readers and significantly improves the hit target size for all users, particularly on touch devices where clicking the label text now focuses the input.
**Action:** Always verify label associations during initial exploration and implement them as a high-impact, low-risk micro-UX win.
